Hey Guys,
For about 1 year 3 months Lokigh has been compiling best times for maps on Avicus into a document on his computer. I am making this post so people can refer to the list of best times and records if curious, but also for people to post new records they have achieved on maps.

To put your time on the list please follow the guideline of; screenshot of map time (displayed on tab), type names of people who got the objectives (if someone gets more than one objective write x2 for example), type the name of the map and previous record.

If valid I will then edit the post and add it to the list :) I do not have screenshots for the times I have currently collected as there are so many, although you will have to trust me that all these times are correct- and I would have no reason to lie anyway.

Also, some of the IGNs on the list are likely to be outdated as this list has been compiled over a long period of time. Enjoy the list and feel free to ask any questions below :)
